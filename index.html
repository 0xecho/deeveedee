How to play
Use the arrow keys or drag the arrow to adjust the direction you want to launch the DeeVeeDee
Press the spacebar to launch the DeeVeeDee
You can only launch once every few bounces. Keep a close watch on the launch meter on the top left of the screen.
The closer to the corner you land, the more points you get
Avoid the red borders, they hurt.
Survive as long as possible, the game ends when your credit runs out.
